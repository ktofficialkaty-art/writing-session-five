1Part 1 地图题概览

1.1 题型分类

动态图（最常见）
  • 一个地方：过去 vs 现在（变化对比）
  • 未来规划 vs 现状
静态图
  • 不同地点的布局对比

1.2 四段式写作框架

Paragraph 1说明地图展示了某地的变化或布局；换词改写，不抄原题。
Paragraph 2概述最主要的变化：什么消失、什么新增、整体趋势。
Paragraph 3描述原图布局，使用 was / had 等合适时态。
Paragraph 4描述变化后的内容，用变化动词和方位词串联。
目标字数：150-200词；6-6.5分目标建议控制在190词以内。

2Part 2 第一段：改写题目

2.1 写作任务

用自己的话说明地图展示了哪个地方、哪个时间段的变化或布局。不能抄原题，需要换词改写。

2.2 常用句式（3选1）

句式一：The maps illustrate the changes in ... between ... and ...

句式二：The diagrams show the layout of ... at present and in the future / how it will look ...

句式三：The maps illustrate the current state of ... and its planned future development.

2.3 换词表

功能可替换表达
展示illustrate / depict / present / demonstrate
变化发展transformation / development / redevelopment
图纸布局diagram / layout / blueprint
现在at present / currently / today / the present day

2.4 范例

原题
The plans below show the layout of a university's sports centre now, and how it will look after redevelopment.
改写 1
The diagrams show the layout of a university sports centre at present and in the future.
改写 2
The diagrams show the layout of a university sports centre at present and how it is planned to look following redevelopment.

3Part 3 第二段：Overall（概述）

3.1 写作任务

概述最主要的变化，不需要细节，抓住2-3个最显著特征即可。

3.2 常用句式

替代式：Overall, it is clear that the area witnessed significant changes, with [old facilities] replaced by [new facilities] respectively.

新增式：The most noticeable change is the construction / removal of [facilities].

没变 vs 变了：While something will remain largely intact, something is set to be expanded / reduced / removed, with new additions including ...

3.3 Overall 常用词汇

变化表达
整体扩展the area expanded significantly / the site was considerably developed
变为住宅区was redeveloped into a residential neighbourhood
变商业化has become more commercial / has been commercialised
现代化has undergone significant modernisation
自然区域缩减natural areas were reduced / replaced by man-made structures

Overall, the most notable change is the removal of both outdoor courts, which will be replaced by a range of new facilities.
While the central indoor zone will remain largely intact,
the sports centre is set to expand significantly, with new additions including a leisure pool, a sports hall, and two dance studios.

The most noticeable additions are a café and a children's play area,

4第三段：描述原图布局

4.1 写作步骤（四步循环）

Step 1先说整体布局（1句）
Step 2聚焦某一板块，说“这里有什么”
Step 3用相对位置引出下一个建筑
Step 4换句式避免重复，再回到 Step 2

Park — Para 3（1920年）

In 1920, the park was accessed via two entrances on Arnold Avenue to the north and Eldon Street to the south.
At the centre of the park stood a fountain, with a stage for musicians positioned to its west.
Three rose gardens were distributed around the western and southern areas, and seating was placed along all four edges.
In the eastern section, a glasshouse sat adjacent to the southern entrance, while a pond for water plants occupied the northeastern corner.

4.2 Step 1 整体布局句式

The ... consisted of ... flanked by ...
The area was divided into ... parts by ...
The ... featured a ... at its centre, with ... on either side.

4.3 Step 2 “这里有什么”句式

句式类型模板例句
主系表sth is/was + 位置The gym was to the north of the pool.
There beThere was/were ... + 位置At the heart of the building, there was a 25m pool.
主动 sit/occupysth sat/occupied ...A gym sat at the northern end of the building.
被动 locatedsth was located/situated ...The reception was located at the southern entrance.

4.4 Step 3-4 相对位置表达系统

介词含义记忆方法
inA 在 B 内部A 被 B 包围
onA 与 B 接壤A 和 B 有共同边界
toA 与 B 分离地图内两建筑之间默认最常用
场景表达例句
紧挨着next to / adjacent to / alongsideThe seating area is adjacent to the pool.
正对面opposite (to) / facing / on the other side ofShops were located opposite the fish market.
方向（分离）to the north/south/east/west ofThe gym is to the north of the pool.
方向（接壤）on the north/south side ofFarmland lies on the north side of the river.
方向（内部）in the northern/southern section ofA café sits in the southern section of the building.
角落（外）at/on the southeastern corner ofA hotel stands at the eastern corner of the junction.
角落（内）in the southeastern corner ofA changing room is in the southeastern corner.
交汇处at the junction/joint ofA hotel sat at the junction of the two roads.
沿线along the river / along the roadHouses were built along both sides of the road.
围绕surrounding / encirclingA road surrounded the housing area.

4.5 四种组合句式（LR 加分）

1. 介词短语 + there be：Opposite to the market, there was a block of shops on the other side of the road.

2. 介词短语 + sit/lie/occupy：East of the shops, a hotel and a café sat at the junction of the two roads.

3. 非谓语作状语：Flanking the central pool on either side, two outdoor courts extended to the east and west.

4. 定语从句带出新事物：The fish market, which was located near the port, sat just south of the main road.

5Part 5 第四段：描述变化

5.1 核心差异

第三段：说清楚“在哪里有什么”。
第四段：说清楚“在哪里，什么变成了什么”。
每句结构：[位置定位] + [原来是什么] + [变化动词] + [现在是什么]

Park — Para 4（变化）

By the present day, the park has been considerably redesigned. To the west, the stage for musicians has been replaced by a large amphitheatre for concerts, which now dominates the western section. A single rose garden is retained in the northwestern corner.
In the centre, the fountain has been removed and a new rose garden constructed in its place, surrounded by seating on all sides.
As for the eastern section, both the glasshouse and the pond have been removed, replaced by a café in the northeast, a children's play area to its east, and a water feature to the south.
Outside the southern entrance, a new entrance to an underground car park has been added.

5.2 变化动词词库

类型动词 / 短语例句
拆除was demolished / was torn down / was removed / was knocked downThe outdoor court was demolished entirely.
新建was constructed / was built / was added / was introducedA sports hall was newly constructed to the east.
扩建was enlarged / was extended / was expandedThe gym will be extended eastward.
改建was converted into / was replaced by / was redeveloped into / was transformed intoThe factory was converted into a residential area.
不变remained unchanged / was retained / remained as it was / stayed the sameThe 25m pool will remain unchanged.

5.3 段内衔接词

功能表达
转移到新区域Turning to ... / Moving to the eastern side ... / As for the northern section ...
并列同类变化Similarly / Likewise / In addition / Also
转折However / While ... remains unchanged / That said
收尾Finally / To complete the transformation
“转移区域”和“并列变化”使用的衔接词不同，混用是 CC 失分点。

5.4 四种高分句式

1. 位置词 + 变化动词：In the southern area, the fishing port was demolished, and the fish market was replaced by apartments.

2. Replacing + 原事物：Replacing the former shops along the roadside, several restaurants now occupy the same location.

3. Where + 原位置描述：Where the fishing port once stood, the coastline has been cleared entirely.

4. While / Whereas：While the 25-metre pool and changing room remain intact, the gym has been extended eastward.

1Discuss Both Views 题目与完整模板

WRITING TASK 2课堂题
Some people think that competition at work, at school and in daily life is a good thing. Others believe that we should try to cooperate more, rather than competing against each other.

Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.

Introduction（三句话结构）

句1 BackgroundIn recent years, the issue of [topic] has sparked widespread debate.
句2 两方观点Some people argue that [A], while others maintain that [B].
Task PreviewThis essay will examine both perspectives before presenting my own view.
句3 Personal StancePersonally, I am inclined to agree with the former / latter view.
Task Preview 不能省：题目要求 Discuss BOTH views；缺少任务预告会削弱 Task Response。个人立场应有倾向，不建议写 both perspectives have their own merits。former 指观点 A，latter 指观点 B。

Body Paragraph 1：你不完全支持的一边

On the one hand, some argue that [View A].
One convincing reason is that [Reason 1].
In addition, [Reason 2 + example / explanation].
That said, this perspective alone may not tell the whole story.

轻收束句表示“我理解这一边，但仍有保留”，并为下一段的倾向观点做过渡。

Body Paragraph 2：你倾向支持的一边

On the other hand, critics of this view point out that [View B].
They argue that [Reason 1 + development].
Moreover, from a long-term perspective, [Reason 2 + example / explanation].
Therefore, I tend to believe that this argument is more compelling.

BP2 必须放你倾向支持的观点；Therefore 收束句是完成 give your own opinion 的关键信号。

Conclusion

In conclusion, although [View A — noun form] may offer certain short-term advantages, I firmly believe that [View B — noun form] is more beneficial for [group / society] in the long run.

结尾用名词形式概括观点，不重复原句，也不引入新观点或新例子。

2开头段写作

背景话题：从题目提取可直接放入句子的名词短语。

In recent years, the issue of competition and cooperation has sparked widespread debate.

合理选择是 competition and cooperation 或 whether to compete or to cooperate；people are going to compete 不是合适的名词话题。

双方观点：不要整句照抄题目，要挑选核心信息重组。

展开开头段范文
Some people argue that people should compete with each other, while others maintain that cooperation has its own advantages.
This essay will examine both perspectives before presenting my own view.
Personally, I am inclined to agree with the former / latter view.

3观点库：Competition

Efficiency / Productivity

思路表达
Core Benefitgreater operational efficiency and higher standards of output across industries
动词建议promotes / leads to / gives rise to
Why（机制）when businesses compete for customers and market share, they are incentivised to reduce costs, improve quality, and innovate continuously in order to survive
Examplethe rivalry between Apple and Samsung in the smartphone industry has produced rapid technological advancement, benefiting consumers with better products at increasingly competitive prices
Long-termcompetitive markets drive sustained innovation and raise the overall quality of goods and services available to the public
市场竞争存在 → 企业必须提升产品质量和效率才能存活 → 消费者获得更好的产品和更低的价格 → 整体行业标准和创新速度持续提升

Personal Growth

思路表达
Core Benefitenhanced emotional resilience and a stronger capacity to handle failure and adversity
动词建议fosters / builds / strengthens
Why（机制）facing competition exposes individuals to setbacks and defeat, teaching them to regulate their emotions, persist under pressure, and develop a growth mindset
Exampleathletes who compete at a high level, such as Olympic participants, consistently report that losing competitions taught them more about discipline and self-improvement than winning
Long-termindividuals who have experienced competitive environments are better equipped to navigate professional challenges and recover from failure throughout their lives
个人参与竞争并经历失败 → 被迫反思不足、调整策略 → 抗压能力和心理韧性增强 → 在职业和生活中面对逆境时更具适应力
Competition 范文：理由 1

On the one hand, some argue that competition is largely beneficial for society.

One convincing reason is that competition promotes greater operational efficiency and higher standards of output across industries. When businesses compete for customers and market share, they are incentivised to reduce costs, improve quality, and innovate continuously in order to survive. For example, the rivalry between Apple and Samsung in the smartphone industry has produced rapid technological advancement, benefiting consumers with better products at increasingly competitive prices.

4观点库：Cooperation

Mental Health

思路表达
Core Benefitimproved psychological well-being and a reduced sense of isolation among individuals
动词建议provides / supports / contributes to
Why（机制）cooperative environments reduce the chronic stress associated with constant performance comparison, allowing individuals to feel valued for their contributions rather than judged solely by their ranking
Exampleresearch in organisational psychology shows that employees in collaborative workplaces report significantly lower levels of burnout and higher job satisfaction compared to those in highly competitive corporate cultures
Long-termworkplaces and schools that foster cooperation produce healthier, more motivated individuals who sustain their performance over the long term rather than burning out under competitive pressure
合作型环境取代竞争型环境 → 个人不再持续与他人比较，压力来源减少 → 焦虑感和职业倦怠显著下降 → 长期心理健康水平提升，持续生产力增强

Education / Career

思路表达

动词建议enables / facilitates / gives rise to
Why（机制）cooperation allows individuals with different skills, backgrounds, and perspectives to pool their expertise, producing solutions and innovations that no single person could generate independently
Examplethe development of the COVID-19 vaccines within a record timeframe was made possible by unprecedented international scientific cooperation, with researchers across dozens of countries sharing data, resources, and findings in real time
Long-termin an increasingly complex world, cooperative approaches to education and professional life produce more well-rounded, adaptable individuals and faster collective progress
